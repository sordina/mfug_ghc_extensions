How do I even??

How do I enable <Extension>?

With GHC Parameters...

foo.hs

 ghc --make -XExtension foo.hs

In Your Source Files...

foo.hs

 {-# LANGUAGE Extension #-} 
 {-# OPTIONS_GHC -XExtension #-}

With Cabal...

foo.cabal

 Extensions:  Extension 
 Ghc-Options: -fextension
